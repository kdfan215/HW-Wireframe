My name is Ryan Braudo and I am 23 years old. I graduated from California Polytechnic State University at San Luis Obispo with a B.S. in Business Administration June 2018. After graduating I decided I wanted to work in the hospitality industry. Apparently, the industry only wants people with experience for entry-level jobs. I love dogs and ended up getting a job at a doggie hotel and worked there for six months. I enjoyed it but the pay was horrendous so it did not work out. I applied for other hospitality jobs for the last 6 months unsuccessfully. That is how I ended up here! I wanted to learn an in-demand skill that will help me no matter the industry. In my spare time I watch NBA basketball. My favorite player is Kevin Durant and my favorite team is the Lakers. I love playing videogames especially Grand Theft Auto and Red Dead Redemption 2. I have two dogs named Bentley and Roscoe. They are good boys. Usually.
